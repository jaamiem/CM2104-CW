About Help

How do I find a space?

  1. Search

    2. Enter your destination.

  2. Filter out

    3. Use the sliders to decide how far you want to search and how much you want to pay.

  3. Choose your space

    4. Pick the space that suits you best from the list!

How do I add a space?

It's easy! Just click the "Add location" button at the bottom of the page and fill out a small form describing the spot!
